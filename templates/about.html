About Our Project

The Loan Approval Prediction System is a web-based Machine Learning application developed using Python, Flask, HTML, CSS, and Scikit-Learn.

This system predicts whether an applicant is likely to receive loan approval based on important financial information. It helps banks and financial institutions make quick, consistent, and data-driven decisions.

Features Used for Prediction

💰

Monthly Income

Applicant's monthly earnings.

📈

Credit Score

Represents financial credibility.

🏦

Loan Amount

Total loan amount requested.

👨‍💼

Years Employed

Employment experience of applicant.

⭐

Points

Additional eligibility score.
